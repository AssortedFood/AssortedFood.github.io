Our Mission

Our vision at AgilePath is to create digital laboratories, improving the quality and reducing the cost of cancer diagnoses for our patients, and aiming to meet the needs of the NHS digital transformation.

The Dream Team

    Christopher Ross

    Co-founder and CEO

    Chris has over 9 years' worth of medical experience and as a practicing pathologist at Imperial College London Hospital. He is an NHS Clinical Entrepreneur developing talent and delivering innovation to the NHS. His core skills are in understanding the clinical needs of patients, doctors, and service providers in the cancer diagnostics process.

    Hamoon Bassiri

    Co-founder and COO

    A senior biomedical scientist with experience working across laboratories in London and Essex. Leveraging his toolkit-oriented software background he has delivered software solutions to five cellular pathology laboratories, providing hundreds of thousands of pounds of value in time savings with digital automation. His core skill is understanding laboratory processes and their digital infrastructure.

    Michael Watts

    Project Manager

    The director of Blüm Health Ltd, bringing novel innovation in healthcare with software. Blüm Health has delivered corporate software projects for the NHS, University of Liverpool, and private organisations. Notably building a COVID-19 solution for reporting staff and patient lateral flow test results; a product that is being used in care homes throughout Scotland.

    Micah Weightman

    Lead Developer

    A senior software developer and the software development lead of our project. He has lead award-winning and market-leading applications handling over £200M of sales per annum.

    James Lomas

    Lead UX & UI Designer

    A Solutions Architect and a UI/UX Designer with experience with successful Point of Sale products, being used by restaurants nationwide. James focuses on the needs of end-users and translates this information to developers enabling the development of technology built for users with growth and scalability in mind.
