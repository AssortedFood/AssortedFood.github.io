Starting out

During the course of our work in Cellular Pathology laboratories, Hamoon and I have experienced, on a daily basis, the delays patients experience with their cancer diagnoses through archaic and paper based systems. We founded AgilePathology with a vision for digital laboratories, using simple, intuitive and Agile software to deliver results to patients with more accuracy and speed.

Change

Unfortunately, current systems in laboratories are slow, inefficient, and reliant on inefficient paper systems. Patients deserve better, faster, higher quality, and lower cost results powered by digital solutions.
This is why we created AgilePath; to create cancer diagnostics software designed for people and transform the delivery of cancer services. At AgilePath we build digital solutions for cellular pathology laboratories creating our vision of a digital laboratory, an efficient and effective ecosystem to serve our patients.
